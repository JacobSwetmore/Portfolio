JACOB SWETMORE

Design.

Hi! I'm Jacob, a Computing Science student from Staffordshire University.

Despite being a Computing Science student, I have a very solid grasp on design. My eye for design stems from my father, who has worked as a Graphic Designer throughout all of my life.

In my time as a photographer, I have accquired a great knowledge of the Adobe suite, working to a professional level with both Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.

I also have experience in working with Adobe Illustrator to create vector graphics. I am fluent with both MacOS and Windows, and I am able to adapt to new software quickly.

I am able to create low, and high fidelity mockups with ease, and possess the programming ability to create a live site from said mockup.

I also have experience working with cutting edge observational labs, in order to master UI/UX Design, for all target audiences.

I have a huge interest in typography, and like stay on top of current design trends.
